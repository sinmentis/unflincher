# Unflincher Domain Language

**Reflection Partner**: The overall product concept. It offers an outside reading of the owner's
journal history and keeps that reading open to challenge in conversation.

**Journal Archive**: The complete set of imported and manually written entries available to
Unflincher.

**Entry Reflection**: An as-of generated reading attached to one journal entry. Its model context
ends at that entry in canonical chronology, so later entries are never visible.

**Life Report**: A cross-year synthesis of recurring patterns, changes, contradictions, and goals.

**Conversation**: Follow-up dialogue about an Entry Reflection, Life Report, or the Journal Archive.

**Perspective**: The globally active stance used for future generation.

**Companion**: A warm Perspective that first reflects emotional reality, then gently broadens the
reading without hiding clear patterns.

**Coach**: A forward-looking Perspective that turns supported patterns into decisions and small next
steps.

**Challenger**: A direct Perspective that names contradiction, avoidance, and moving excuses without
attacking identity or humiliating the owner.

**Analyst**: A precise, minimally editorial Perspective that separates observation from
interpretation and offers little unsolicited advice.

**Custom**: User-authored instructions that do not exactly match a shipped Perspective preset.

**Prompt Workshop**: The place to choose a Perspective, edit instructions, select a model, preview one
entry, and apply the result.

**Entry Reference**: A dated journal entry named as support for a generated interpretation. It is not
an independently verified citation.
